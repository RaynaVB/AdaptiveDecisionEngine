# ✨ COPY STYLE GUIDE (IMPORTANT)

## Voice = AI Health Coach (not app)

### Use:

* “You”
* cause → effect
* confident but not absolute

### Avoid:

* “We recommend…”
* “Consider…”
* generic language

---

## Examples

### ❌ Weak

“Consider checking Health Lab for experiments”

### ✅ Strong

“Late meals are disrupting your sleep — test this for 5 days”

---

# 🧬 PERSONALIZATION RULES

Everything on homepage should adapt based on:

* Goals (energy, sleep, etc.)
* Symptoms (bloating, fatigue, etc.)
* Active experiments
* Time of day

---

## Example (Morning vs Night)

### Morning

* “Start your day with protein to stabilize energy”

### Night

* “Avoid eating now to protect sleep”

---

# 🔥 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

## 1. One decision > many options

User should act in **< 5 seconds**

## 2. Reduce cognitive load

* max 5 sections
* max 2 alerts
* 1 primary action

## 3. Stability

* don’t reshuffle aggressively
* build trust
