## Granularity rules

Keep separate when the distinction matters:
- onion vs garlic
- milk vs cream vs yogurt
- soy sauce vs miso
- wheat flour vs almond flour

Collapse when the distinction usually does not matter:
- red onion -> onion
- yellow onion -> onion
- vine tomato -> tomato
- baby spinach -> spinach
